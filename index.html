Aritra Bhowmik

​I am a PhD candidate at the Video and Image Sense (VIS) Lab at the University of Amsterdam, under the supervision of Cees G. M. Snoek. My research interests encompass computer vision, with a focus on vision-language models, video understanding, and object detection. I obtained my master's degree in artificial intelligence from the Indian Institute of Science, Bangalore, where I worked under the guidance of Chandra Sekhar Seelamantula on image super-resolution.

Following my master's, I joined the Computer Graphics and Visualization (CGV) group at TU Dresden, collaborating with Stefan Gumhold on a project with FSD . Our team developed a platform for testing self-driving car algorithms.

Currently, my research focuses on video understanding and video generation.
